Who We Are

A Seed of Trust, a Harvest of Hope

Krishisetu Seeds Pvt. Ltd. — literally meaning "Bridge of Agriculture" — was founded with one purpose: to be the most trusted partner of the Indian farmer. From research labs to remote villages, our seeds carry the promise of better yields, higher incomes and stronger rural communities.

We combine traditional agricultural wisdom with modern science. Every variety we release is tested across multiple agro-climatic zones, and every bag is processed through strict quality protocols — so that the farmer sows with confidence and reaps with joy.

  • ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management
  • Strict adherence to The Indian Seeds Act, 1966
  • R&D-driven seed selection and breeding
  • Strong dealer network across 15+ states

Vision, Mission & Values

The principles that guide every seed we produce and every relationship we build.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted seed brand by 2030, empowering 1 million farmers with high-performing, scientifically-bred seed varieties.

Our Mission

To deliver pure, high-germination, disease-resistant seeds backed by research, quality control and farmer-first service — at a price every farmer can afford.

Our Values

Honesty in every grain, quality in every bag, respect for the farmer, responsibility towards the land — these are the values that root us.
